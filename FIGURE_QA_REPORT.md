## Bug Found and Fixed — Fig10 Panel (b)

**Finding:** Fig10 (Z-Statistic Comparison Matrix) panel (b) "MMK | Z-statistic" was completely blank (no cell values, empty heatmap) in both the archived figure and the freshly reproduced version.

**Root cause:** In `rta/figures/method_comparison.py`, `_METHOD_KEYS[1]` was set to `"Modified MK (H&R98)"`, but `rta/batch.py` stores the method label as `"Modified MK"` (without the citation). The filter `df[df["Method"] == "Modified MK (H&R98)"]` returned an empty DataFrame, producing a blank panel.

**Fix applied:** Changed `_METHOD_KEYS[1]` from `"Modified MK (H&R98)"` to `"Modified MK"` and updated `_METHOD_SHORT` to match. No Z values, p-values, or any scientific result was modified.

**Verification:** Regenerated Fig10 from checkpoint data (`ckpt_04_trends.pkl`). Panel (b) now shows 36 MMK Z-statistics (12 stations × 3 scales) with correct values consistent with S2 sheet of WB1.

**Archive updated:** `results/archive_figures/primary_pipeline/Fig10_ZComparisonMatrix.png/.pdf` replaced. Checksums in `checksums.sha256` and `figure_manifest.csv` updated. All 66 checksums verified OK.
